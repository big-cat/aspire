A LOOK BACK AT 2015

1. 4,600 people took on the Aspire Channel Swim 2015 in their local pools

2. £420,000 raised by participants so far

3. You can take part in any pool you like - 2015's swimmers took part in 15 countries, from the Falkland Islands to the United Arab Emirates

4. The most popular female name was Sarah, the most popular male name was Paul and the most popular surname was Smith

5. The funds raised so far could buy: 140 lightweight wheelchairs, or 1,200 SmartNav computer adaptations, or 420 housing adaptations

6. The youngest participant was 5 and the oldest was 87

7. The average age of participants was 38

8. 78% of participants were female, and 22% were male
